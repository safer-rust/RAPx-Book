# Chapter 8.5. Limitations

- **Path enumeration** is capped at 1024 paths per callsite. Functions with very complex control flow may exceed this limit.
- **Inter-procedural analysis** is limited to pre-defined call summaries; full cross-function MIR-level verification is not supported.
- **SMT encoding** approximates numeric constraints. Non-linear arithmetic (multiplication of two variables) may yield `Unproved`.
- **Standard library contracts** are maintained manually; functions not covered are silently skipped.
- **Not all property kinds** have dedicated SMT checkers; unrecognized tags produce `Unknown` and do not block soundness.
- **Postfix repeat convergence** may not exhaustively unroll deeply nested SCCs. Off-by-one bugs requiring more than 16 loop iterations may escape detection.
- **Concurrent code** and **inline assembly** are not analyzed; the analysis assumes single-threaded execution.
- **Trait implementation verification** is deferred (logged during `--prepare-targets` but not yet verified).
